OPIUM

OPIUM LABEL is a record label and creative agency based in Atlanta, Georgia. It was founded and curated by Playboi Carti and Opium Baby in 2019. Artists on the label include: Playboi Carti, Destroy Lonely, Homixide Gang and Ken Carson.

Jordan Terrell Carter, professionally known as Playboi Carti, is a recording artist from Atlanta, Georgia. Carti began his musical journey in 2011 as Sir Cartier. He changed his artist name to Playboi Carti in May 2013 and joined Awful Records. In 2014 Carti moved to New York where he would become acquainted with fashion tastemakers Ian Connor and A$AP Bari, who introduced Carti to A$AP Rocky at SXSW in March 2015. Under Interscope and AWGE, Carti released his long-awaited debut commercial mixtape Playboi Carti on April 14, 2017. After months of garnering buzz and momentum off of his successful debut mixtape, Carti released his debut studio album Die Lit on May 11, 2018. In 2019, Carti founded and curated Opium: his record label and creative agency based in Atlanta, Georgia. Two and a half years after releasing his debut studio album, Carti announced that his anticipated sophomore studio album, Whole Lotta Red, would be released on Christmas Day of 2020.

Kenyatta Lee Frazier Jr., professionally known as Ken Carson, is an American recording artist and record producer from Atlanta, Georgia. He gained attention in the underground rap scene and signed to Opium, the label founded by Playboi Carti, in 2019. Ken released his first two EPs, "Boy Barbie" and "Teen X" in 2020. His breakout single, "Yale" was featured on the "Teen X" EP. In 2021, he released the first Lost Files compilation on SoundCloud and his third EP, "Teen X: Relapsed." Ken's debut studio album, "Project X," was released in July 2021 and achieved major success, with songs like "Rock N Roll" and "Change" gaining attention on social media. This led to Ken Carson signing a deal with Interscope Records in 2022. He released the third installment of Lost Files on SoundCloud, featuring collaborations with Lil Tecca and Destroy Lonely, and followed it up with his sophomore album, "X" in July 2022. The album became another success, with songs like "MDMA" "Murda Musik" and "Freestyle 2" gaining popularity. Ken Carson's third studio album, "A Great Chaos" was originally set to be released in July 2023 but was delayed due to label issues. It was eventually released on October 13, 2023. Ken has also been involved in production work, including a placement on Lil Uzi Vert's album "Pink Tape." After the release of "A Great Chaos" Ken teased the possibility of a deluxe version of the album.

Bobby Wardell Sandimanie III, known as Destroy Lonely, is an American recording artist from Atlanta, Georgia. He adopted his stage name based on personal experiences and emotions. "Destroy" represents his belief that drugs were destroying him during high school, while "Lonely" reflects his feelings of isolation during online schooling.He gained a following through SoundCloud releases and formed relationships with artists in the Atlanta music scene, collaborating closely with producer Clayco and being associated with the Underworld Music Group. In December 2020, Lonely caught the attention of Playboi Carti after releasing the music video for his song "Oh Yeah" He subsequently signed with Carti's label, Opium, in early 2021. Since then, Lonely has gained mainstream attention, performing at festivals and collaborating with other artists. His highly anticipated mixtape, "NO STYLIST" was released in 2022, along with the single "NOSTYLIST" which boosted his popularity on social media. He later signed with Interscope Records. In 2023, Lonely released his biggest single to date, "if looks could kill" which gained attention on platforms like TikTok even before its official release. His debut studio album, "if looks could kill" achieved commercial success upon its release. He followed up with a Director's Cut version of the album featuring new tracks. Lonely's music is characterized by otherworldly and ominous instrumentals, complemented by his dark and ambient vocal delivery, adding to his mysterious persona.
